MicroShield

"A creative space to explore new innovations in the field of biotechnology with projects underpinned by commercial cause" 

MicroShield was establsihed in 2018 by a student looking to test and explore new untouched boundaries in the world of Biology. When science meets business, a truly amazing nexus of two subjects, teaming with high risks and rewards becomes a single discipline that most often sees the fallen fate of many who attempt. At MicroShield, we battle these odds to harness the natural capacity of microorganisms for the benefit of humanity. Through hard work and persistence, we have set-up projects that span all three domains of life, with immense support from university academics and funding from the Laidlaw Scholarship, Summer Accelerator and the Proof of Concept Fund.  

Research Projects

Creating a novel biological algae control specific to aquaria. Here, we are looking to harness competitive microbial interactions to reduce the overgrowth of algae and ensure a clean and healthy tank for users.

Algae Control

An ammonia-oxidising archaea supplement which kick-starts the nitrification cycle in newly setup aquariums, preventing new tank syndrome i.e. the loss of fish upon their introdcution into a newly setup tank

Archaea Nitrifcation.

Creating biological paints. At this primary stage we are testing the capacity to extract sage green pigments from penicillium digitatum - This project falls strongly into the circular economy.

Bio-Paints

Research Aims

Innovation

Our work is highly creative. We believe that through innovation, new technologies surrounding biology can be designed and created to better humanity.

Results

We aim to achieve results; for us a result is a positive impact, no matter how big or small on an individual’s life.

Enterprise

The work we conduct underpins a commercial cause. We are driven entrepreneurs who aspire to apply our acumen to solve real world problems.

Communication

Communication is at the heart of our ethos. We enjoy our practice and are eager to share our work with the greater community.
